Project Information

Rusheen House
Location: Foxrock, Co. Dublin
Year: 2021–2022
Role: Architectural Graduate
Stage: Sketch Design, Planning/Appeal
Contribution: Worked with a Director to develop design options, planning drawings, 3D models and visual material. All visualisations shown were produced by me.

Description

Residential design and planning project for Rusheen, an early 20th century gate lodge on Westminster Road within the Foxrock Architectural Conservation Area. The project involved testing different design approaches for the site, including a completely new house option and an alternative proposal to retain and extend the existing house.

The project required careful consideration of conservation context, scale, roof form, neighbouring properties, site access, drainage/SuDS and planning appeal issues. My work focused on developing the 3D model, preparing design option material, producing visualisations, and assisting with planning and appeal stage drawings and visualisations.

Key Responsibilities

Developed 3D models and visualisations for design development and client review
Assisted with design option studies for both new house and extension/refurbishment approaches
Prepared and updated planning/appeal drawings and presentation visual reports
Assisted with drawings and visual material for planning and appeal stage documentation
Gained experience in residential design within an Architectural Conservation Area, including conservation context and mature sites
