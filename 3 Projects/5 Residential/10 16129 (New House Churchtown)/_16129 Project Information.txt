Project Information

Private Residence
Location: Churchtown, Dublin
Year: 2018–2019
Role: Architectural Assistant
Stage: Construction Stage
Contribution: Assisted during the construction stage, Selected drawings shown were prepared or updated by me as part of the project documentation process. All visualisations shown were produced by me.

Description

Large private residential project in Dublin 14, involving the construction of a new detached two storey over basement dwelling with ancillary accommodation, landscaped external areas and associated site works.

My involvement was during the construction stage, with a particular focus on the design development and detailing of the basement leisure areas. This included spaces such as the gym, cinema, pool area and home bar. The work required careful coordination of internal layouts, finishes, junctions, lighting, services and detailed design information to support the construction team on site.

This project gave me early practical experience of high end residential construction and the level of detail required to bring internal leisure and amenity spaces from design intent through to built form.

Key Responsibilities

Assisted with construction-stage drawing updates and detail design information
Developed basement layout and detail drawings for gym, cinema and home bar areas
Prepared internal design information for finishes and built-in elements
Supported construction stage design development as the works progressed on site
Gained experience in high end residential interiors and construction stage detailing
