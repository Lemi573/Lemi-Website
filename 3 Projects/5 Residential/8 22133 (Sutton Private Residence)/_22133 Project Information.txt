Project Information

Sutton Private Residence
Location: Sutton, Co. Dublin
Year: 2023–2024
Role: Architectural Graduate
Stage: Sketch Design, Planning, Tender, Construction
Contribution: Worked under Director oversight through sketch design, planning, tender and construction stages. All visualisations shown were produced by me.

Description

High end residential upgrade project to a large private house in Sutton. The works included internal refurbishment and M&E upgrades to the main house, a new detached gym building, a garden pavilion and new landscaping.

The project focused on improving the house and garden as a whole, with new leisure spaces, upgraded interiors and a stronger connection between the main living areas and the rear garden. The garden works included level changes, paving, planters, external seating areas, BBQ/fireplace elements and outdoor amenity spaces.

Key Responsibilities

Prepared and updated sketch design, planning, tender and construction stage drawings
Assisted with internal upgrade works to the main house, including finishes, M&E, joinery
Coordinated garden and external works information, including stone finishes, planters, external seating and BBQ/fireplace elements
Gained experience in high end residential refurbishment
