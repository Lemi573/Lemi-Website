Project Information

Location: Killeen Road, Dublin

Role: Project Architect

Short Description

Internal fit-out and joinery upgrade at Toyota Ireland’s headquarters on Killeen Road, Dublin. The project included customer reception upgrades, bespoke joinery, first floor office alterations, internal finishes, electrical/data coordination and branded internal elements. Unlike the dealership rollout projects, this was focused on Toyota Ireland’s own office headquarters environment, requiring careful coordination of joinery, finishes, services and works within an occupied corporate setting.

Key Responsibilities

Led project coordination through design, tender, construction and handover
Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated specialist joinery, finishes, electrical/data and associated fit-out works
Reviewed shop drawings, submittals and site queries
Carried out site inspections, snagging and handover coordination
