Project Information

LAB Castlebar Fit-Out
Location: Castlebar, Co. Mayo
Year: 2025
Project Value: Approx. €0.9m construction cost
Role: Architect
Stage: Planning, Tender, Construction & Handover
Contribution: Acted as Architect through planning, tender and construction stages. Selected drawings shown were prepared by me or developed collaboratively with Directors and internal team members.

Description

Public sector office fit-out for the Legal Aid Board at Castlebar, involving the conversion of an existing first-floor commercial unit into a regional law centre. The project included new offices, meeting rooms, client facing areas, staff facilities, internal partitions, new ceilings, joinery, firestopping, mechanical/electrical services and associated support spaces.

This project had a strong construction stage coordination focus as several client requested changes were incorporated during construction, requiring drawing updates, technical coordination and practical resolution of layout, finishes and services-related items. A separate planning application was also prepared for external air conditioning units serving the new office and consultation spaces.

As Architect I coordinated architectural information through planning, tender and construction stages, including planning documentation, tender/construction drawings, RFI responses, submittal reviews, site inspections, snagging and handover support. The project also included a Fire Officer inspection during the works, with a positive outcome and the fire safety works accepted on site.

Key Responsibilities

Prepared and updated tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Liaised directly with the contractor during construction to respond to RFIs, submittals and site queries
Coordinated client requested changes during the works, including drawing updates and technical design coordination
Coordinated architectural information with mechanical, electrical, fire safety, accessibility and fit-out requirements
Attended site meetings, carried out site inspections and provided architectural input during the works
Coordinated  snagging, handover and close-out of architectural items
