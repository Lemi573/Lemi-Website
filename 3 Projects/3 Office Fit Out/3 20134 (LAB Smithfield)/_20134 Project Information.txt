Project Information

LAB Smithfield Fit-Out
Location: Brunswick Street, Dublin 7
Year: 2024–2025
Project Value: Approx. €2.3m construction cost
Role: Architect
Stage: Sketch Design, Tender, Construction, Completion & Handover
Contribution: Acted as Architect from sketch design through tender, construction and handover. Selected drawings shown were prepared by me or developed collaboratively with Directors and internal team members.

Description

Public sector fit-out and alteration project for the Legal Aid Board’s Smithfield Law Centre. The project involved the phased refurbishment of an existing multi-storey building to upgrade public-facing law centre accommodation, office areas, meeting rooms, staff facilities, support spaces, services infrastructure and circulation areas.

As Architect I was involved from sketch design through tender, construction and handover. The works were delivered across multiple phases, including upper floor renovations, roof-level works, ground floor reconfiguration, staircore works and basement/plant-related items. The building remained in use during the project, requiring careful phasing, staff relocation planning, access coordination, escape route coordination, landlord/building management coordination and continuous consultant input.

The project was delivered under a Public Works Contract. My role focused on architectural design coordination, tender and construction documentation, construction-stage drawing updates, architectural responses to site queries, site inspections and snagging.

Key Responsibilities

Prepared sketch layouts, tender drawings, construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated phased works across multiple floors, including upper floor renovations, roof-level works, ground floor reconfiguration and staircore works
Coordinated architectural information with mechanical, electrical, fire safety, accessibility, security, landlord and operational requirements
Responded to architectural site queries, contractor information requests and construction-stage drawing revisions
Attended site meetings, carried out site inspections and provided architectural input during the works
Reviewed progress information and construction stage submittals relating to partitions, services, ceilings, raised access floor and internal fit-out works
Prepared snag lists and coordinated architectural completion items through to handover
Provided architectural input alongside the Employer’s Representative during construction
