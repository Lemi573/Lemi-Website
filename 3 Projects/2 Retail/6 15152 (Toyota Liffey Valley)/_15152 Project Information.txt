Project Information

Toyota Liffey Valley
Location: Liffey Valley, Dublin
Year: 2024
Role: Project Architect / Project Coordination
Stage: Sketch Design, Tender, Construction, Snagging & Handover

Short Description

Retail concept upgrade of Toyota Liffey Valley as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout. The project included a substantial showroom joinery package, customer hub, consultation pods, welcome and service desks, frame wall/hero screen elements, handover area, kitchenette/coffee dock, AV and M&E coordination, together with external signage and associated site works. The project required careful coordination between joinery, signage, AV, electrical, plumbing and builder’s works within an existing dealership environment.

Key Responsibilities

Led project coordination through sketch design, tender, construction and handover
Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated the main contractor and specialist packages, including joinery, signage, AV and M&E works
Reviewed shop drawings, submittals and site queries
Coordinated showroom joinery elements, including customer hub, consultation pods, welcome/service desks, framewall and handover area
Coordinated external signage works, including existing sign removals, facade screen changes, foundations and civil works
Carried out site inspections, snagging and handover coordination
