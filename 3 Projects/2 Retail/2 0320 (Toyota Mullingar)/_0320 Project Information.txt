Project Information

Toyota Mullingar
Location: Mullingar
Year: 2025
Role: Project Architect
Stage: Sketch Design, Tender, Construction, Snagging & Handover

Short Description

Large retail upgrade of Grange Motors Toyota Mullingar as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project included a significant showroom and sales office fit-out, AV coordination, new internal glazing screens, parts area alterations, external signage, and associated civils/structural coordination. The works required careful coordination between internal fit-out, specialist joinery, signage, AV, M&E and main contractor's building works.

Key Responsibilities

Led project coordination through tender, construction and handover
Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated the main contractor and specialist packages, including joinery, signage, AV and M&E works
Reviewed shop drawings, submittals and site queries
Prepared RIAI payment certificates based on QS recommendations
Carried out site inspections, snagging and handover coordination
