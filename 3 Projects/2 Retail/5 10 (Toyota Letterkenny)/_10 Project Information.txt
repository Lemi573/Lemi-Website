Project Information

Toyota Letterkenny
Location: Letterkenny, Co. Donegal
Year: 2025
Role: Project Architect
Stage: Sketch Design, Tender, Construction, Snagging & Handover

Description:

Retail upgrade of Kelly’s Toyota Letterkenny as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project included external signage upgrades, showroom joinery, AV coordination, finishes and associated building works. As Project Architect, I led coordination from sketch design through construction, including tender documentation, specialist package coordination, site inspections, snagging and handover.

Key Responsibilities

Led project coordination through design, tender, construction and handover
Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated signage, joinery, AV and contractor interfaces
Reviewed shop drawings, submittals and site queries
Carried out site inspections, prepared payment certificates based on QS recommendations, snagging, handover
