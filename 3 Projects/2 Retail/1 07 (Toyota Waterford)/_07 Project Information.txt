Project Information

Toyota Waterford
Location: Waterford
Year: 2024–2025
Role: Project Architect
Stage: Sketch Design, Tender, Construction, Snagging & Handover

Description

Upgrade of Tom Murphy Car Sales, Waterford as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout. The project involved refurbishment of the existing showroom and offices, upgraded finishes, service department works, external signage bases, cladding/flashings upgrades and coordination of client-direct joinery and signage packages. The works were carried out within an existing operational dealership, requiring careful coordination between the main contractor, specialist suppliers and the client.

Key Responsibilities

Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated showroom, office, service department and external signage scope
Liaised with the main contractor, QS, client and specialist signage/joinery contractors
Reviewed shop drawings, submittals and site queries during construction
Prepared RIAI payment certificates based on QS recommendations
Carried out site inspections, snagging and handover coordination
