Project Information

Toyota Long Mile
Location: Long Mile Road, Dublin
Year: 2025
Role: Project Architect
Stage: Sketch Design, Tender, Construction, Snagging & Handover

Description

Signage upgrade of Toyota Long Mile Road as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project focused on the removal of existing external signage and installation of new Toyota signage, including facade screen, totem signs, exterior archway, welcome sign, lightbox, location signage and associated foundation works. The project required careful coordination of signage, civils, electrical and structural requirements within an existing live dealership site.

Key Responsibilities

Led project coordination through tender, construction and handover
Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated signage scope, including removals, new signage, foundations and electrical trenching
Liaised with QS, signage contractor, civil/structural engineer and client
Reviewed signage drawings, submittals, foundation details and site queries
Coordinated site-specific issues, including foundation requirements and existing site constraints
Carried out site inspections and snagging
