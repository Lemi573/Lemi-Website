Project Information

Toyota Blackrock
Location: Dublin
Year: 2025
Role: Project Architect
Stage: Tender, Construction, Snagging & Handover

Description:

Retail upgrade of Toyota Carroll & Kinsella Blackrock as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project included showroom alterations, joinery fit-out, AV coordination, external signage works, entrance door upgrades, M&E coordination and associated refurbishment works. As Project Architect, I coordinated the project through tender, construction and completion stages.

Key Responsibilities:

Led project coordination through tender, construction and handover
Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated the main contractor and specialist packages, including joinery, signage, AV and M&E works
Reviewed shop drawings, submittals and site queries
Prepared RIAI payment certificates based on QS recommendations
Carried out site inspections, snagging and handover coordination
