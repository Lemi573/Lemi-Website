Project Information

Toyota Kilkenny
Location: Kilkenny
Year: 2025–2026
Role: Project Architect
Stage: Sketch Design, Tender, Construction, Snagging & Handover

Short Description

Retail upgrade of Walsh’s Toyota Kilkenny as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project involved showroom reconfiguration, sales office upgrades, AV and power/data coordination, external signage and associated builder’s works. The proposal required careful coordination of internal fit-out works with external signage, foundations and existing dealership operations.

Key Responsibilities

Led project coordination through tender, construction and handover
Prepared tender/construction drawings, schedules and supporting documentation
Coordinated the main contractor and specialist packages, including joinery, signage, AV and M&E works
Reviewed shop drawings, submittals and site queries
Prepared RIAI payment certificates based on QS recommendations
Carried out site inspections, snagging and handover coordination
