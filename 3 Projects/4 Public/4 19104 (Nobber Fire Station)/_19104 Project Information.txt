Project Information

Nobber Fire Station
Location: Nobber, Co. Meath
Year: 2021–2023
Project Value: Approx. €1.2m construction value
Role: Architectural Graduate
Stage: Tender, Construction & Handover
Contribution: Worked alongside a Director during the construction stage, assisting with construction-stage documentation, drawing updates and technical queries and coordination of selected architectural items. Selected drawings shown were updated by me as part of the project documentation process. All photographs shown were captured by me.

Description

Public works project involving the extension and upgrade of Nobber Fire Station in Co. Meath, including improvements to the existing building, new extension areas, external works and associated site upgrades to support fire service operations.

I was involved as an Architectural Graduate during the construction stage, working alongside a Director to assist with technical coordination, drawing updates, contractor queries and construction stage information. The project involved a number of detailed architectural items, including external zinc cladding, fitted furniture and specialist stairs.

The project gave me valuable experience in public sector construction projects and emergency services buildings and the level of coordination required between the architect, contractor, consultants, QS and local authority client.

Key Responsibilities

Assisted the Director with construction stage architectural coordination
Prepared and updated drawings and technical information during the works
Supported responses to contractor queries and construction stage coordination items
Gained experience in public sector procurement and emergency services buildings
