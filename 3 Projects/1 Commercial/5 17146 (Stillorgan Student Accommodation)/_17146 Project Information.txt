Project Information

Stillorgan Road Student Accommodation
Location: Stillorgan Road, Co. Dublin
Year: 2018–2019
Role: Architectural Assistant
Stage: Planning, FSC/DAC, Tender & Construction
Contribution: Assisted senior architects with revised planning, FSC/DAC, tender and construction drawings. Selected drawings shown were prepared/updated by me as part of the project documentation process. All visualisations shown were produced by me. 

Description

Redevelopment of a former B&B/guesthouse on Stillorgan Road into purpose built student accommodation. The project originally received planning permission for conversion and extension of the existing building, but following structural investigations the scheme was revised to demolish the existing structure and provide a new student accommodation building. A further revised application was later prepared to increase the building to three storeys, providing 24 student bedspaces with shared kitchen & living facilities.

My role included assisting with revised planning drawings, tender and construction stage updates and FSC/DAC documentation as the project evolved through multiple permissions and design changes. I worked alongside senior architects to update drawings in line with revised layouts, Building Regulations requirements and planning conditions. I also produced visualisations and 3D walkthrough videos for marketing purposes before the building was completed.

Key Responsibilities

Assisted with revised planning drawings and supporting documentation
Updated FSC and DAC drawings to reflect changes to the approved scheme
Reviewed relevant Building Regulations TGDs as part of FSC/DAC drawing updates
Supported tender & construction stage drawing revisions following changes to the permitted design
Assisted with 7 Day Notice documentation to allow works to progress on site
Coordinated drawing updates with a senior architect during planning and compliance stages
Gained experience in student accommodation, planning conditions, fire/access compliance and local authority procedures
