About

I am a Registered Architect MRIAI based in Ireland, with over five years of experience across commercial, retail, office fit-out, public/civic and residential projects.

My work has included planning applications, design development, tender and construction documentation, consultant coordination, site-stage support and project delivery through to completion. I have worked on a wide range of projects, from Toyota dealership upgrades and public sector fit-outs to fire stations, residential extensions, one-off houses and mixed-use developments.

Alongside architectural coordination and technical documentation, I have a strong interest in 3D visualisation. I regularly produce 3D models, CGI images, photomontages, shadow studies and walkthrough material to support design development, planning submissions and client presentations.

This portfolio brings together selected projects from my professional experience, showing my involvement across different project types, stages and scales.
