Retail upgrade of Toyota Carroll & Kinsella Blackrock as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project included showroom alterations, joinery fit-out, AV coordination, external signage works, entrance door upgrades, M&E coordination and associated refurbishment works. As Project Architect, I coordinated the project through tender, construction and completion stages.
