Large retail upgrade of Grange Motors Toyota Mullingar as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project included a significant showroom and sales office fit-out, AV coordination, new internal glazing screens, parts area alterations, external signage, and associated civils/structural coordination. The works required careful coordination between internal fit-out, specialist joinery, signage, AV, M&E and main contractor's building works.
