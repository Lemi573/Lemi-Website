Signage upgrade of Toyota Long Mile Road as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout across Ireland. The project focused on the removal of existing external signage and installation of new Toyota signage, including facade screen, totem signs, exterior archway, welcome sign, lightbox, location signage and associated foundation works. The project required careful coordination of signage, civils, electrical and structural requirements within an existing live dealership site.
