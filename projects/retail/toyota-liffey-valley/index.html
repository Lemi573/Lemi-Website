Year

Role

Stage

Retail concept upgrade of Toyota Liffey Valley as part of the Toyota National Retail Concept rollout. The project included a substantial showroom joinery package, customer hub, consultation pods, welcome and service desks, frame wall/hero screen elements, handover area, kitchenette/coffee dock, AV and M&E coordination, together with external signage and associated site works. The project required careful coordination between joinery, signage, AV, electrical, plumbing and builder’s works within an existing dealership environment.
